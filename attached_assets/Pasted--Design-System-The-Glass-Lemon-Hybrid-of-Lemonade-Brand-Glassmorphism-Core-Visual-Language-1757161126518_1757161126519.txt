🍋 Design System: The Glass Lemon

Hybrid of Lemonade Brand + Glassmorphism

🌐 Core Visual Language

This system emphasizes transparency, depth, and brightness.

Glassmorphism: frosted cards, blur effects, layered depth

Lemonade branding: bold yellow as a primary accent

Clarity first: always maintain high contrast for text readability

Floating feel: generous whitespace, subtle shadows
